# KMD Sitemap and Page Requirements

## Overview
This sitemap turns the brainstorm into a practical frontend scope for KMD's bilingual construction e-commerce website. It assumes a mixed model: service lead generation plus product discovery and future online ordering.

## Primary Navigation
- Home
- Services
- Products
- Brands
- Portfolio
- Blog or Resources
- About
- Contact
- Search
- Account
- Cart

## 1. Home
Purpose: introduce KMD, direct users to services and products, and build trust.

Requirements:
- Hero banner with `Explore Services` and `View Products`
- Short company introduction
- Featured services section
- Featured product categories
- Highlighted brands
- Featured projects or gallery preview
- Why choose us section
- Quick inquiry form
- Footer with contact details, links, and social channels

## 2. Services Listing
Purpose: show KMD service categories and route users into detailed service pages.

Requirements:
- Service category cards
- Short service descriptions
- Visual thumbnails or icons
- Inquiry CTA on each card

## 3. Service Detail Page
Purpose: explain one service clearly and capture leads.

Requirements:
- Service overview
- Service gallery
- Scope of work or deliverables
- Related materials or products
- FAQ
- Inquiry form with project type, location, and preferred date

## 4. Product Catalog
Purpose: let users browse products by category, brand, or use case.

Requirements:
- Category and brand filters
- Price and availability filters
- Grid and list views
- Sort controls
- Product cards with image, price, and quick actions
- Pagination or load more

## 5. Product Detail Page
Purpose: provide full product information and conversion actions.

Requirements:
- Image gallery
- Product title, SKU, brand, and category
- Variant selector if applicable
- Pricing for standard and future B2B scenarios
- Technical specifications
- Related products
- Reviews section
- Add to cart and inquiry actions

## 6. Search
Purpose: help users find products, brands, and services quickly.

Requirements:
- Autocomplete
- Result grouping by products, services, and brands
- Search results page with filters
- No-results guidance

## 7. Cart
Purpose: review selected products before checkout.

Requirements:
- Item list with quantity controls
- Variant details
- Pricing summary
- Remove and update actions
- Coupon field
- Continue shopping and checkout CTAs

## 8. Checkout
Purpose: capture address, payment, and order confirmation data.

Requirements:
- Step-based checkout
- Customer information
- Address selection or entry
- Order summary
- Payment method selection
- Confirmation screen

## 9. Account Area
Purpose: support returning customers and B2B buyers.

Requirements:
- Login and registration
- Profile management
- Address book
- Order history
- Wishlist
- Inquiry history if service leads are tracked

## 10. Brand Pages
Purpose: present supplier or brand information and connect users to related products.

Requirements:
- Brand overview
- Brand logo and visuals
- Product listings by brand
- Contact or inquiry CTA

## 11. Portfolio
Purpose: show completed projects and build trust.

Requirements:
- Project gallery
- Category filters such as residential and commercial
- Project detail pages with images and summary

## 12. Blog or Resources
Purpose: support SEO and educate customers.

Requirements:
- Article listing
- Article detail page
- Topics such as materials, installation, and buying guides

## 13. About
Purpose: communicate company identity and credibility.

Requirements:
- Company overview
- History
- Mission
- Vision
- Key strengths

## 14. Contact
Purpose: provide direct communication channels.

Requirements:
- Contact form
- Phone and email
- Office location map
- Business hours

## 15. Global Requirements
- Khmer and English content support
- Mobile-responsive layout
- SEO-friendly page structure
- Clear trust signals for construction and B2B buyers
- Performance-conscious image handling
- Accessible forms, buttons, and navigation

## Priority Build Order
1. Home
2. Services listing and service detail
3. Product catalog and product detail
4. Search
5. Cart and checkout
6. Account area
7. Brands, portfolio, blog, about, and contact
